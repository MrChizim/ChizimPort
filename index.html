Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does it take to build a website?

Personal websites typically take 5-7 days, family websites 10-15 days, and business websites vary depending on complexity. I prioritize quality while maintaining efficient timelines.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage my website?

Not at all! I build user-friendly websites that are easy to manage. I'll provide you with all the guidance you need to make simple updates, and I'm always available for support.

What if I need revisions?

Your package includes standard revisions during the development process. Additional revisions after delivery are available at ₦15,000 per revision to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! All websites I build are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. Your site will look stunning on any screen size.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

Yes! Domain and SSL certificate services are available as an optional add-on for ₦60,000 - ₦70,000/year. This is not compulsory, and you can choose your own hosting provider if preferred.
